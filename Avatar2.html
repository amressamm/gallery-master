About the Characters

Jake Sully is an ex-Marine who is recruited into the RDA’s Avatar program after his scientist twin-brother, Tommy, is killed. Since Jake and Tommy share identical DNA make-up, Jake is able to step into his deceased brother’s role as an Avatar driver. His consciousness is passed from his human body through the Eye of Eywa and into his avatar body. When he opens his eyes, he is reborn as a Na'vi.
