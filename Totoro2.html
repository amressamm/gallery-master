Characters

Totoro

A large, mystical forest spirit that lives in the forset near Satsuki and Mei's house that later becomes their friend.

Satsuki

The older sister, around 10, she is resposible and energetic, she often takes care of Mei when their father is at work.

Mei

Satsuki's 4-year-old sister, Mei is curious, adventrous and imaginative. Her innocent nature leads her to discover Totoro, sparking the magical journey for both the sisters.
